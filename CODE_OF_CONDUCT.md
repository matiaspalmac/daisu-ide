# Code of Conduct

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any communication channel where the project is represented.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful. Disagree about code, not about people.
- Assume good faith. Most misunderstandings are not malice.
- Stay on topic. Issues track work; off-topic threads get closed.
- Credit contributors. Reviews critique the diff, not the author.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks, slurs, or sustained hostility.
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent.
- Spam, off-topic promotion, or repeated derailment.
- Any conduct a reasonable contributor would find unwelcoming.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, lock, or remove content and may block contributors who violate this code. Decisions are final but can be appealed by email to the maintainer listed in `SECURITY.md`.

## Reporting

Report violations privately to the maintainer email in `SECURITY.md`. Reports stay confidential.
